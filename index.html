How GitHelp works

From posting a job to getting it done — the whole process is fast, clear, and stress-free.

01
📋

Post your job

Choose a service, pick your date or select ASAP, and tell us a little about the job. Takes under 2 minutes.

02
🔍

A worker applies

Qualified, vetted workers in your area see your job and apply. You see their profile and ratings before deciding.

03
✅

Job gets done

Your worker completes the job. You confirm with a photo, approve the work, and you're done. No cash needed.

04
⭐

Rate your worker

Leave a rating so your neighbors know who the best workers are. One flat price — no tips, no surprises.
