Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

githelp ("we", "our", or "us") operates the githelp mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, phone number, and optional email address
  • Identity verification: government-issued ID and selfie, processed by Stripe Identity
  • Payment information: processed securely by Stripe — we never store card details
  • Location: used to show nearby jobs and approximate job areas
  • Photos: before and after job photos uploaded by workers
  • Push notification tokens: used to send job updates and alerts
  • Profile photo: optional photo uploaded by workers

How We Use Your Information

  • To connect customers with local workers for home service jobs
  • To process payments and payouts via Stripe
  • To verify the identity of all platform users
  • To send push notifications about job status and updates
  • To resolve disputes between customers and workers

Independent Contractor Relationship

Workers on githelp are independent contractors, not employees. githelp is a marketplace platform that facilitates connections between customers and workers.

Data Retention

After a job is completed and the 24-hour review window closes, we purge chat messages, job photos, and address information. We retain job title, city/state, completion date, payment amount, and star ratings for record-keeping purposes.

Third-Party Services

  • Stripe: payment processing and identity verification — stripe.com/privacy
  • Twilio: SMS verification — twilio.com/legal/privacy
  • Supabase: database and authentication — supabase.com/privacy
  • Expo: push notifications — expo.dev/privacy

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at githelpapp@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

githelp is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at githelpapp@gmail.com.
